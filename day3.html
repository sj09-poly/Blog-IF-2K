Visit Elephant Beach offering calm blue shallow waters and is the perfect spot for water sports lovers It’s also a great place to just spend the day lying on the beach and relaxing.
The reef here starts very close to shore at a depth of about 1 meter. The large reef is mostly made up of hard coral and has plenty of marine life.
One can enjoy various water sports activities on the beach.
